Strathmore University

Vision

To become a leading outcome-focused entrepreneurial research university, providing quality education and generating solutions that drive socio-economic development in Africa.

Mission

To serve the Kenyan society and the world by developing visionary leaders who are ethical, lifelong learners and catalysts for change through world-class teaching, research, and innovation.

Achievements

  • Ranked top private university in Kenya by Webometrics (2025).
  • Accredited by the Commission for University Education and ISO 9001:2015 certified.
  • Over 40,000 alumni making impact across 60+ countries.
  • Centre for Sustainability Leadership awarded the 2024 Green Gown Award (Africa).

Ongoing Projects

Academic & Research

  • Digital Learning Initiative – expanding e-learning infrastructure and course offerings.
  • AI4D Africa Hub – a research collaboration on responsible AI for development.
  • Solar Micro-Grid Lab – pioneering renewable-energy micro-grid solutions.

Student-Centred

  • Strathmore Innovation & Technology Park (iBizAfrica) expansion.
  • Green Campus Project – zero-waste and carbon-neutral campus by 2030.
  • Taita–Taveta Community Health Outreach – annual medical camp led by medical students.
